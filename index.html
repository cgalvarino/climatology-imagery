Welcome to the SECOORA CLIMATOLOGY tool where you can view and download historical temperature and salinity data from as well as . Use the panels to the right of the graph to explore the possibilities. You may zoom and pan the interactive graph, and use your mouse to hover over lines to view point information. Please be aware when viewing buoy results and when viewing model results. The information below the map will update to reflect the data sources used to create the graph. If your browser supports Flash, you will be able to download the graph data. For more information about this application, visit our GitHub repository. We welcome your comments and questions. Contact us at communications@secoora.org.
